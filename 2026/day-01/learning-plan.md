# What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?
DevOps and Cloud Engineering are complementary disciplines in modern IT, focusing on accelerating software delivery and managing scalable, secure infrastructure, respectively. DevOps emphasizes automation, CI/CD pipelines, and collaboration to bridge development and operations teams. Cloud Engineering centers on designing, implementing, and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP).
